About Me

My name is Mercy Chukwuemeka, and I'm a software developer born and raised in Lagos, Nigeria. I discovered programming when I was trying to customize my Blogspot page back in secondary school — and honestly, I haven't stopped coding since.

I graduated from the University of Lagos with a degree in Computer Science. Since then, I've worked with startups across Africa and remotely with international teams building products that solve real problems — from fintech platforms handling millions in transactions to EdTech tools helping students across West Africa.

When I'm not at my desk debugging some stubborn API, you'll find me at tech meetups in Yaba, mentoring junior developers, or arguing about jollof rice superiority (Nigerian jollof is the best, no debate 🍚).

I believe technology can transform Africa, and I'm committed to being part of that story. Every line of code I write is a small step towards building a better digital future for our continent.
